Minnesota

Restaurant

Good Earth Restaurant
Location: 1901 MN-36, Roseville, MN 55113
Contact Info: (651) 636-0956
Website: https://goodearthmn.com/
About: The Good Earth restaurant prides itself in only serving the best quality health foods. The homey venue serves veggie options as well. The food is healthy, colorful, and wholesome. Google reviews gave this spot 4.3 stars and locals love this place.

Living Quarters

Sheraton Bloomington Hotel
Location: 5601 W 78th St, Minneapolis, MN 55439
Contact Info: (952) 835-1900
Website: https://www.marriott.com/
About: This hotel is located in a populated and central area of Minnesota. Located only 7 miles from the Mall of America, the rooms offer flat screen TV's and free Starbucks coffee. The hotel also has 11,750 square feet of meeting space.

Landmark

Mall of America
Location: 60 E Broadway, Bloomington, MN 55425
Contact Info: (952) 883-8800
Website: https://www.mallofamerica.com/
About: As the name suggests, the Mall of America is the largest mall in America. with 4 levels and hundreds of stores, you would want to spend the day here exploring. What makes this mall so special is that there is a full sized theme park inside.

Spa

Chase on the Lake
Location: 502 Cleveland Blvd W, Walker, MN 56484
Contact Info: (218) 547-7777
Website: https://www.chaseonthelake.com/
About: This lakeside resort is built to be conveniently walking distance from the national forest and casino. The spa has great service and there are many activities to do outside of the spa.

Museum

Science Museum of Minnesota
Location: 120 W Kellogg Blvd, St Paul, MN 55102
Contact Info: (651) 221-9444
Website: https://www.smm.org/
About: The Science Museum of Minnesota houses prehistoric dinosaurs and will provide you with the most hands-on experience a museum can give you. Crowds can form around noon so try to get there early to avoid lines and long waits.
