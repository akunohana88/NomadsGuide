Colorado

Restaurant

Work & Class
Location: 2500 Larimer St, Ste 101, Denver, CO 80205
Contact Info: (303) 292-0700
Website: https://www.workandclassdenver.com
About: Work & Class is a restaurant that features latin american cuisine, with vegan and vegetarian options for non meat-eaters. It’s a casual dinner place with a full bar and wonderful service. You should definitely stop by here before you leave Colorado.

Living Quarters

The Oxford Hotel
Location: 1600 17th St, Denver, CO 80202
Contact Info: (303) 628-5400
Website: http://www.theoxfordhotel.com
About: This historic hotel provides a day spa and complimentary access to a public health center for all guests, and the decor is classically chic. Established in 1891, it’s one of the first Lower Downtown hotels in Denver. It’s a good place to stay if you’re looking for a comfortable sleeping arrangement in Colorado.

Landmark

Colorado State Government Capitol
Location: 200 E Colfax Ave, Ste 136, Denver, CO 80203
Contact Info: (303) 866-2604
Website: http://leg.colorado.gov
About: The legislative capital of Colorado is a great place to tour to see some of the history of Colorado, as well as the history of the building itself. The famous Mile High step is exactly one mile higher than sea level, and many visitors like to come to this spot to take pictures of the Rocky Mountains, which can be seen from that spot. It’s a great place to visit if you want to learn more about Colorado before you leave the state.

Spa

Salon Foushee & Spa
Location: 1400 Market St, Ste 200, Denver, CO 80202
Contact Info: (303) 955-2020
Website: https://www.fousheesalonspa.com
About: This spa offers facials, waxings, massages, skin care, and other pampering-oriented beauty regimens, as well as various hair styling services. If you’re looking for a moment of relaxation during your travels, the services of the Salon Foushee & Spa are definitely something you’ll want to experience before you leave Colorado.

Museum

Denver Museum of Nature & Science
Location: 2001 Colorado Blvd, Denver, CO 80205
Contact Info: (303) 370-6000
Website: http://www.dmns.org
About: With an IMAX 3D theater, a planetarium, and numerous fascinating science exhibits, the Denver Museum of Nature & Science is a wonderful place to vee while you visit Colorado. Feel free to bring your children here to experience the museum that was first created in 1900.
