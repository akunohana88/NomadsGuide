Georgia

Restaurant

Community Smith
Location: 866 W Peachtree St NW, Atlanta, GA 30308-1123
Contact Info: (678) 412-2367
Website: https://www.communitysmithatl.com/
About: Community Smith is a moderately priced restaurant in Atlanta, Georgia. One of the things that makes this retaurant so special is their live jazz band that performs on Friday nights. The service is impeccable and it serves breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

Living Quarters

Hotel Indigo Atlanta-Vinings
Location: 2857 Paces Ferry Rd SE, Atlanta, GA 30339-3793
Contact Info: 877-859-5095
Website: https://www.ihg.com/hotelindigo/hotels
About: Hotel Indigo in Atlanta, Georgia is a beautiful botique hotel with elegant furnishings. You will be sure to love the historic Vinings feel. Free shuttle transportation to and from the Atlanta Braves Stadium and Cobb Galleria Center are also available.

Landmark

World of Coca-Cola
Location: 121 Baker St NW, Atlanta, GA 30313
Contact Info: (404) 676-5151
Website: https://www.worldofcoca-cola.com
About: You have probably heard of the popular fizzy drink known as Coca-Cola. Well now you can visit this very place to see how they make their famous soft drink. You will be sure to find lots of history about this company founded in 1886.

Spa

Buckhead Grand Spa
Location: 3338 Peachtree Rd, Atlanta, GA 30326
Contact Info: (404) 816-4511
Website: http://www.buckheadgrandspa.com
About: Unwind and relax at the Buckhead Grand Spa. With a reasonable walking distance from the Community Smith restaurant, this spa is so convenient to go to after a nice meal. They offer seaweed wraps, waxing, and special facials. Prices can get expensive so I recommend that you invest in their packages.

Museum

Georgia Museum of Art
Location: 90 Carlton St, Athens, GA 30602
Contact Info: (706) 542-4662
Website: http://georgiamuseum.org
About: Calling all art lovers! The Georgia Museum of Art is definitely the place to go. The gallery is open to the public on Tuesday's, Wednesdays, Friday's, and Saturday's. Make sure to stop by the museum gift shop and get some souvenirs to commemorate your trip in Georgia.
