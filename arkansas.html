Landmark

Mammoth Spring State Park
Location: 17 US Hwy 63, Mammoth Spring, AR
Contact Info: N/A
Website: http://www.arkansasstateparks.com/mammothspring/
About: Located in the town of Mammoth Spring, the Mammoth Spring State park is one of the largest springs in the USA. The water here flows at approximately nine million gallons per hour. Also in the park is a 1886 train depot and a Frisco Railroad caboose.
