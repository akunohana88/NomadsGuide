Location: 2500 West End Ave, Nashville, TN 37203
Contact Info: (615) 862-8431
Website: https://www.nashville.gov/
About: This replica of the Parthenon in Athens, Greece is absolutely breathtaking. The beautiful columns and carefull designed architecture houses some of the most famous works of art. It opens from Tuesday to Sunday, but times can vary so be sure to check the website for more information.
