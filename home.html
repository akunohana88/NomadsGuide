About Nomad's Guide

Are you looking to travel the US, but not sure where to start? Visiting a state and you don't know where you want to go? Are you just generally curious about what things the US has to offer? If you answered yes to any of these questions, have no fear! This site is for you. Simply look up any state that you want to visit to see our recommendations for the top five things you should do before you leave. Now's the time to endulge your dreams of traveling across the US; after all: if not now, when?
