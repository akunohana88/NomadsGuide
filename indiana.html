Indiana

Restaurant

3 Days in Paris
Location: 222 E Market St, Indianapolis, IN 46204
Contact Info: (317) 912-0072
Website: http://www.3daysinpariscrepes.com
About: This street style creperie located in Indiana serves sweet and savory crepes. Crepes range from 4-8 dollars. The most popular crepe is 'The Gustave' which has banana, nutella, and walnuts. This market is open Monday through Saturday from 8 am to 2 pm so make sure you get here early.

Living Quarters

The Westin Indianapolis
Location: 241 W Washington St, Indianapolis, IN 46204
Contact Info: (317) 262-8100
Website: https://www.marriott.com/
About: The Westin Indianapolis has so much to offer. Since it is located in downtown Indiana, the view of the street lights is breathtakingly beautiful. The airy rooms, steakhouses, pool, gift shop, and fitness center are just a few things this hotel has to offer.

Landmark

Kurt Vonnegut Museum and Library
Location: 340 N Senate Ave, Indianapolis, IN 46204
Contact Info: (317) 423-0391
Website: https://www.vonnegutlibrary.org
About: Explore the world of the famous write Kurt Vonnegut. The library and museum serve as a cultural and educational resource. At the museum you can see the exact model of typewrite he used and his drawings as well as rejection letters from editors.

Spa

The Woodhouse Day Spa - Carmel

Contact Info: (317) 706-1300
Website: https://www.woodhousespas.com

Museum

The Children's Museum of Indianapolis
Location: 3000 N Meridian St, Indianapolis, IN 46208
Contact Info: (317) 334-4000
Website: https://www.childrensmuseum.org
About: This is the world's largest children's museum located in the United Northwest area. There are four floors including a lower level exhibit that houses a planetarium, dinosphere, and water clock. The museum is open 7 days a week from 10 am to 5 pm.
