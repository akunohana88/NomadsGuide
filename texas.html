Living Quarters

The Houstonian Hotel
Location: 111 N Post Oak Ln, Houston, TX 77024
Contact Info: (713) 680-2626
Website: http://www.houstonian.com
About: This hotel is packed with amenities for you to enjoy while you stay. The Houstonian Club is a huge exercising facility that allows hotel patrons access to three pools, a boxing ring, and a rock climbing wall, among other things. The hotel also has a spa where you can get massages, facials, manicures, pedicures, and hairstylings. With friendly staff, comfortable rooms, and free Wifi, this is definitely a place to consider if you’re looking for a hotel in Texas.

Landmark

Big Bend National Park
Location: Big Bend National Park, TX
Contact Info: (432) 477-2251
Website: http://www.nps.gov
About: There’s ton of activities to engage in in this national park. Hiking excursions, scenic drives, river trips, and bicycling are just a handful of the activities that can be done within the canyons in this location. It’s a cool place to visit, if you can.

Spa

Zalla Massage
Location: 1411 Haddon St, Houston, TX 77006
Contact Info: (281) 974-2823
Website: https://www.ZallaMassage.com

Museum

Houston Museum of Natural Science
Location: 5555 Hermann Park Dr, Houston, TX 77030
Contact Info: (713) 639-4629
Website: http://www.hmns.org
About: The Houston Museum of Natural Science is a cool location to explore tons of science exhibits. There’s an entire floor dedicated to dinosaur bones, as well as a butterfly conservatory where real butterflies are flying around, to name a few of its offerings. It’s a great place for you and your family to go while you’re in Texas.
