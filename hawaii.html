Hawaii

Restaurant

Marukame Udon
Location: 2310 Kuhio Ave, Honolulu, HI 96815
Contact Info: (808) 931-6000
Website: https://www.toridollusa.com
About: Marukame Udon is a Japanese restaurant that serves udon, primarily, and with a combination of wonderful cuisine and low prices, this place is a hit among all who frequent it. Swing by to try some of their Curry Udon or Niku Udon before you leave Hawaii.

Living Quarters

The Kahala Hotel & Resort
Location: 5000 Kahala Ave, Honolulu, HI 96816
Contact Info: (808) 739-8888
Website: http://www.kahalaresort.com
About: This resort is filled with great amenities for its guests, from a spa, a fitness center, and 5 restaurants to its complementary surfing and paddleboarding lessons and its proximity to the Waialae Beach Park and the Kahala Mall Center. It’s a great place to stay if you want a comfortable room with free Wifi and other accommodations, as well as some other activities in or near the hotel to participate in while you’re there.

Landmark

Diamond Head State Monument
Location: Diamond Head Rd, Honolulu, HI 96816
Contact Info: (808) 587-0300
Website: http://dlnr.hawaii.gov/dsp
About: This location can be hiked up to between 30 minutes to an hour, at which point you will get a magnificent view of the Waikiki beach, as well as Honolulu. It’s a great activity to undertake in if you’re into hiking and seeing beautiful views from the three lookout points on the trail; just be sure to come early to beat the crowds and the heat.

Spa

Red Earth Massage & Wellness
Location: 400 Hobron Ln Ste 211, Honolulu, HI 96815
Contact Info: (808) 942-4325
Website: https://www.redearthmassage.com
About: Red Earth Massage & Wellness is a great place to come and get a massage, with friendly staff and reasonable prices; not to mention the fact that the massages themselves are extremely relaxing. If you’re looking for a good place to get some pampering before your trip comes to a close, this is definitely a place to consider.

Museum

Honolulu Museum of Art
Location: 900 S Beretania St, Honolulu, HI 96814
Contact Info: (808) 532-8700
Website: http://www.honolulumuseum.org
About: This museum has a variety of installations from familiar artists, such as Vincent van Gogh and Andy Warhol, collections of traditional Asian and Hawaiian art pieces, and even a theater where films and concerts are performed. It’s a great place to come if you’re looking for some great art, plus it’s kid-friendly.
