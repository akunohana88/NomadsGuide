Iowa

Restaurant

Zombie Burger
Location: 300 E Grand Ave, Des Moines, IA 50309
Contact Info: (515) 244-9292
Website: https://www.zombieburgerdm.com
About: The Zombie Burger is a hipster burger joint in Des Moines that tons of people seem to love. It’s both vegetarian and vegan friendly, and it’s great for kids and groups. With a quirky sci-fi/horror-themed menu and a full bar, it’s an awesome place to visit.

Living Quarters

Des Lux Hotel
Location: 800 Locust St, Des Moines, IA 50309
Contact Info: (515) 288-5800
Website: http://www.desluxhotel.com
About: This is a wonderful hotel complete with high end decor, complimentary breakfast, and a fitness bar, among other things. It’s a great place to stay while you’re in Iowa.

Landmark

Old Capitol
Location: 21 Old Capitol, Iowa City, IA 52242
Contact Info: (319) 335-0548
Website: http://www.oldcap.uiowa.edu
About: The Old Capitol Museum was once the legislative capital of the state of Iowa, although now it is a part of the University of Iowa. It’s an interesting piece of history with various exhibits and workshops about the history of Iowa, and it’s also a cool place to check out while you stay in Iowa.

Spa

East Village Spa
Location:601 E Locust, Ste 202, Des Moines, IA 50309
Contact Info:(515) 309-2904
Website: https://www.evdayspa.com
About: This relaxing spa is a great place to come for massages, manicures, and pedicures, to name a few things. It has a warm, inviting environment, and the staff is super friendly. Come make an appointment to get a massage before you leave the state!

Museum

Des Moines Art Center
Location: 4700 Grand Ave, Des Moines, IA 50312
Contact Info: (515) 227-4405
Website: http://www.desmoinesartcenter.org
About: The Des Moines Art Center has a ton of beautiful art in a variety of mediums, from sculpture to paintings. It’s child-friendly and free for the public to view, so it won’t cost you anything to come here. Why not pop in for a bit and see what’s there?
