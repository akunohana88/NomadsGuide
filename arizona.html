Landmark

Montezuma Castle
Location: Montezuma Castle Rd, Camp Verde, AZ
Contact Info: (928) 567-3322
Website: N/A
About: Everyone knows about the Grand Canyon, which is arguably Arizona’s most famous landmark, but this historical attraction is not as widely known. The Montezuma Castle was once home to the Sinagua tribe over six hundred years ago, and is still in immaculate condition. This five-story cliff dwelling is also an official national monument located in Camp Verde. Anyone who happens to be nearby should visit this locale in order to see a wonderfully preserved piece of history.

Museum

Musical Instrument Museum
Location: 4725 E Mayo Blvd, Phoenix, AZ 85050
Contact Info: (480) 478-6000
Website: http://mim.org/
About: The Musical Instrument Museum in Arizona is a fantastic place to see musical instruments from all around the world. It regularly holds concerts and has a variety of activities for children of all ages. This is one of the most positively rated museums in all of Arizona, and is considered to be one of the 20 best museums in the United States. Wth reviews like this, there’s no reason not to pop in and look around if you ever find yourself nearby.
