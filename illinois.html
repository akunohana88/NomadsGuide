Landmark

Navy Pier
Location: 600 E Grand Ave, Chicago, IL 60611
Contact Info: (312) 595-7437
Website: https://navypier.org/
About: Navy Pier is located on the Chicago shoreline of Lake Michigan. It is a great place to visit with the family. You can take boat rides and complete life-sized mazes. Don't forget to ride the Ferris Wheel and enjoy your walk on the boardwalk.

Location:9603 Woods Dr, Skokie, IL 60077
Contact Info: (847) 967-4800
Website: https://www.ilholocaustmuseum.org/
