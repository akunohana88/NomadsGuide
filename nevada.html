Nevada

Restaurant

Gordon Ramsay Hell's Kitchen
Location: 3570 S Las Vegas Blvd, Las Vegas, NV 89109
Contact Info: (702) 731-7373
Website: caesars.com
About: If you're a fan of Gordon Ramsay's show Hell's Kitchen then you will enjoy the replica of it right here in his very own Caesar's palace. Serving dishes inspired by his hit TV show it is truly a one of a kind experience. Make sure to make reservations as wait lists can get long.

Living Quarters

The Linq
Location: 3535 South Las Vegas Boulevard
Contact Info: (800) 634-6441
Website: https://www.caesars.com/linq
About: The Linq hotel located on the famous Las Vegas Strip is an amazing hotel which houses its own casino and luxury rooms. From most of the rooms you can get a clear view of Vegas' High Roller, a view you would not want to miss.

Landmark

Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area
Location: 1000 Scenic Loop Dr, Las Vegas, NV 89161
Contact Info: (702) 515-5350
Website: https://www.redrockcanyonlv.org
About: The Grand Canyon is known for its geological features such as towering red sandstone peaks and the Keystone Thrust Fall. There are 13 miles of panoramic viewing spots that dot the Scenic Drive. Make sure to bring your camera to take lots of pictures.

Museum

The Mob Museum
Location: 300 Stewart Ave, Las Vegas, NV 89101
Contact Info: (702) 229-2734
Website: https://themobmuseum.org
About: The Mob Museum located in downtown Las Vegas is a nonprofit organization with a mission to advance the public understanding of organized crime's history. It offers a bold and authentic view of American crime from the back alleys of American cities.
