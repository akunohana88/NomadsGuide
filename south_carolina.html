Museum

The Charleston Museum
Location: 360 Meeting St, Charleston, SC 29403
Contact Info: (843) 722-2996
Website: http://www.charlestonmuseum.org/
About: Along with many fossils, animal mounts, and specimen, this museum also has many historical houses.
