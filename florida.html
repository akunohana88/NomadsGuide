Restaurant

Blue Water Bay
Location: 319 FL-26, Melrose, FL 32666
Contact Info: (352) 475-1928
Website: http://www.thebluewaterbay.com/
About: Blue Water Bay is a seafood restaurant and all-you-can-eat buffet on Friday and Saturday nights. According to locals, the food and service is very good and the wine list is extensive. Don't forget to save room for desert.

Living Quarters

LEGOLAND Florida Resort Hotel
Location: 1 Legoland Way, Winter Haven, FL 33884
Contact Info: (888) 690-5346
Website: https://www.legoland.com/
About: This Lego themed hotel will bring you back to your childhood. This 5-story colorful hotel is perfect if you have children. It will be sure to bring your imagination to life and spark creativity. Parking is free in August and there are deals on their website for discounts when you book a room.

Museum

The Dali Museum
Location: 1 Dali Blvd, St. Petersburg, FL 33701
Contact Info: N/A
Website: https://thedali.org
About: The Salvador Dali Museum houses some of the most amazing artwork painted by the artist himself. You can also ask for a complimentary audio tour that will guide you through the history of his art. Make sure to leave yourself a couple of hours to get the full experience.
