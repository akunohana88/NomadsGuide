Rhode Island

Restaurant

Los Andes Restaurant
Location: 903 Chalkstone Ave, Providence, RI 02908
Contact Info: (401) 649-4911
Website: https://www.losandesri.com
About: The Los Andes Restaurant is a casual place that serves tons of Latin American cuisine at affordable prices. The waitstaff is friendly, the food is great, and there’s even a full bar. There’s no reason not to stop by and grab a bite here while you’re in Rhode Island.

Living Quarters

The Dean Hotel
Location: 122 Fountain St, Providence, RI 02903
Contact Info: (401) 455-3326
Website: http://www.thedeanhotel.com
About: This affordable hotel is a great place to rest your head while you’re staying in Rhode Island. The staff is extremely helpful, the decor is artsy/vintage, and there’s even a coffee shop, a karaoke bar, and a speakeasy-esque bar right within the hotel. It’s also dog-friendly, so your canine companions can accompany you on your stay!

Landmark

The Breakers
Location: 44 Ochre Point Ave, Newport, RI 02840-6906
Contact Info: (401) 847-1000
Website: http://www.newportmansions.org
About: The Breakers is a mansion belonging to the wealthy Vanderbilt family, whose fortune was built upon steamships and the New York Central Railroad during the 19th century. The house, which has been designed in the Renaissance style, is now a National Historic Landmark, and it properly demonstrates the extreme wealth and social status held by the Vanderbilts during this time period. It’s a beautiful piece of history that you should definitely visit if you have the chance.

Spa

Harmony on Hope Massage
Location: 335 Hope St, Providence, RI 02906
Contact Info: (401) 270-88776
Website: https://www.harmonyonhopemassage.com
About: Harmony on Hope Massage is an affordable, relaxing place to get pampered while you’re in Rhode Island. The environment is extremely inviting, and the staff is extremely skilled at their jobs. It’s a great place to go for both relaxation and the treatment of some of the physical ailments.

Museum

RISD Museum
Location: 224 Benefit St, Providence, RI 02903
Contact Info: (401) 454-6500
Website: http://www.risdmuseum.org
About: The Rhode Island School of Design Museum was established in 1877, and features a host of famous historical artists, like Picasso and Matisse, as well as some modern day artists. When you come here, you’ll have access to over 100,000 art pieces from all throughout history, which is why you should make it a point to stop by before you leave Rhode Island.
