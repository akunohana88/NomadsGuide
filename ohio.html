Landmark

Carillon Historical Park
Location: 1000 Carillon Blvd, Dayton, OH 45409-2023
Contact Info: (937) 293-2841
Website: http://www.daytonhistory.org
About: The Carillon History Park is a good place to learn about the History of Dayton, Ohio while you visit the state. It has exhibits on the Wright brothers and the 1913 flood, plus there’s a gift shop, a cafeteria where you can eat, and walking grounds all around the area.

Spa

Bellazio Salon & Day Spa
Location: 101 E Alex Bell Rd, Dayton, OH 45459-2753
Contact Info:(937) 432-6722
Website: https://www.bellaziosalondayspa.com
About: The Bellazio Salon & Day Spa offers a bunch of treatments, from various skin care regimens and make-up services to massages and mani-pedis. You should definitely come here if you’re looking for a place to relax while you’re in Ohio.

Museum

National Museum of the U.S. Air Force
Location: 1100 Spaatz St, Wright-Patterson Air Force Base, Dayton, OH 45433-7102
Contact Info: (937) 255-3286
Website: http://www.nationalmuseum.af.mil
About: This museum is a great place to see all sorts of exhibits from the US Air Force, like Presidential planes and planes by the Wright brothers, and even non-American planes, like Russian MiGs and WWI/WWII-era German planes. There’s tons to explore in their really cool exhibits, so why not pop in and look at a few?
