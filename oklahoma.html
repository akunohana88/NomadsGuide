Restaurant

The Melting Pot
Location: 4 E Sheridan Ave, Oklahoma City, OK 73104
Contact Info: (405) 235-1000
Website: https://www.meltingpot.com/
About: This restaurant is a fondue restaurant that contains multiple courses, including a first course that consists of gourmet cheeses, a main course which incorporates steak and seafood, and ending off with salads and signature desserts, such as chocolate fondue.

Living Quarters

Renaissance Waterford Oklahoma City Hotel
Location: 6300 Waterford Blvd, Oklahoma City, OK 73118
Contact Info: (405) 848-4782
Website: https://www.marriott.com/hotels/travel/okcbw-renaissance-waterford-oklahoma-city-hotel/
About: This four star hotel is surrounded by many destinations for tourists to attend such as the Oklahoma City Zoo and the Penn Square Mall. Not only does this hotel have places to go to outside, the hotel itself includes a 24 hour fitness center, a outdoor pool, and event venues.

Landmark

Oklahoma City Zoo
Location: 2101 NE 50th St, Oklahoma City, OK 73111
Contact Info: (405) 424-3344
Website: https://www.okczoo.org/
About: There are many attractions and wild encounters at this zoo where the tourists can get to know many of the different species. For example, there is a giraffe feeding platform, elephant presentations, sea lion presentations, and much more. This zoo also has many rides such as an endangered species carousel and a children’s zoo water stream.

Spa

Renaissance Salon & Spa
Location: 6205, 10440 Broadway Ext, Oklahoma City, OK 73114
Contact Info: (405) 751-8888
Website: http://salonsparen.com/

Museum

Zoozeum
Location: 2101 NE 50th St, Oklahoma City, OK 73111
Contact Info: (405) 424-3344
Website: https://www.okczoo.org/
About: This one of a kind museum is located within the the Oklahoma City Zoo. Guests inside the museum will be able to see two galleries, the Main Gallery and the Train Gallery, where there will be displays about the Zoo’s past.
